What is Patent Risk Radar?

Patent Risk Radar is an app where you can explore AI‑related patents and assess their potential for dual‑use risks (technology that can be used for both civilian and military purposes) and civic risks (impacts on privacy, fairness, transparency, and human rights).

Our platform helps researchers, policymakers, and innovators identify patents that may pose unintended societal harm, enabling proactive governance and responsible innovation.
